Features

Sales

Create Sales Invoices with ease. User-friendly invoice templates, just add your business logo for a custom touch. Recovery and unpaid balances are always at hand.

Purchases

Have a bird's-eye view of all expenses and purchases. Record Bills, with expenses or products. Manage and email Purchase Orders. Unpaid Bills and Payments with one click.

Cash & Bank

Maintain Cash Book digitally, full record of payments, receipts and transfers in bank or cash ledgers. Manage Cashflow and perform Bank Reconciliation

Inventory

Fully automated inventory module to manage stock and cost of sale. Multiple Units of Measuremnet for each product along with Multi Warehouse inventory.

Project Accounting

Multiple projects at the same time, each project has full reporting, project cash flows, nominal activities and detailed profit and loss account.

Assembly

The manufacturing process and cost management made so easy, start a job, select raw materials and costs, once done, just finish the job to get full cost details.

Income Tax Withheld

Easy to keep track of Advance Income Tax deduction by Customers, option to deduct Income Tax on Supplier Payments, integrated FBR Payments for Withholding Tax.

Sales Tax (GST)

Fully compliant with Sales Tax legislation. Filer and Non-Filer GST management. Withholding GST Tax on Supplier Payments. Sales Tax Return information with one click.

Advance Users Rights

Multi-Users with advanced Role Management. Detailed user Rights at the module level, Select from View / Add / Edit / Delete. Control Access to Banks, Locations and Customers.

Reports

Advance Reports Module with 150+ Financial Reports, giving you full insight into every aspect of your business. Management Reports always Up to date.
